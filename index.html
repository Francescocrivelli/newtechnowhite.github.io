Profile Description

Francesco Crivelli, the only child of Gloria Patricia Gonzalez and Aldo Crivelli, was born in Mendrisio Switzerland on October 21, 2002. His educational training was carried out at the Arturo Del Oro-Valparaiso Scuola, until the 3rd year of secondary education, passing with excellence.


Francesco at an early age shows great interest in science, motivated by astronomy books, space has a passion in his life. At the age of 11, he started his first robotics workshops at the USM, a world that unveiled over time, and led him to be part of a series of national and international competitions. Representing the Robotics Club -USM; Educational robotics from the Science and Technology program of the Mustakis Foundation; National Robotics Selection Program-National Science and Innovation Community (Con-Ciencia-Los Chilis-FablabUC). Also at home, presents great interest and concern for electronics and electricity, which fortunately are channeled by their active and persistent participation, in the Academy of Electronics Technology of the USM School of Electronics.
